About me

Web Developer

Welcome to my portfolio site! I am thrilled to introduce myself as a prospective graduate of the AEC program in web design at Collège de Maisonneuve. With a genuine passion for the web, I am dedicated to creating exceptional user experiences that are both simple and intuitive. Transforming design concepts into dynamic and responsive realities brings me great satisfaction. My mission is to contribute to the success of your projects by developing efficient and reliable code, always following the latest web best practices.

Let me share a bit about my background. Holding a bachelor's degree in administration with a specialization in service management, I have gained valuable experience working in established companies throughout my professional journey. Alongside this, I discovered my profound love for photography, which quickly transformed into a fulfilling profession. However, a desire for a career that would unite my creative spirit with my strong organizational and managerial skills led me to embark on a path of change. With this in mind, I made the decision to return to school and undergo training in web development.

I am elated with my choice and resolute in my commitment to this field. In addition to my technical prowess, I bring a sense of responsibility, impeccable organizational abilities, a collaborative nature, meticulous attention to detail, and the wisdom accumulated throughout my professional endeavors. I am an individual driven by a relentless thirst for knowledge and an unwavering enthusiasm for exploring new horizons.

Thank you for visiting my portfolio site. I look forward to the opportunity to collaborate and create remarkable digital experiences together.
